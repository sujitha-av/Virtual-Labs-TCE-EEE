HALF ADDER

Aim Theory Procedure simulation Postquiz Outcome Feedback
  • The addition of 2 bits is done using a combination circuit called a Half adder.
  • Half Adder's design is very simple. It is the basic building block for single bit addition.
  • Just with an inverter gate, Half-Adders can be converted to Half Subtractors.
  • The half adder is not fit for carrying the addition of the previous operation since it has two inputs. Hence, it will not apply in conducting multibit operations.
  • The binary addition property of half adders is applicable in the working of calculators.
