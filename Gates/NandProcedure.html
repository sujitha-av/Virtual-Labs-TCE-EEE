NAND GATE

Aim Theory Procedure Simulation Postquiz Outcome Feedback
  • Observe the IC-7400 and its switchboard on the left side and the internal circuit diagram of the NAND gate on the right side.
  • Select the desired INPUT PIN(A) from the dropdown provided in the switchboard.
  • The INPUT PIN(B) and the OUTPUT PIN(Y) will change with respect to the INPUT PIN(A).
  • Turn on the "VCC" to start the experiment.
  • Give Logic 1 (ON) or LOGIC 0 (OFF) to the INPUT(A) and INPUT(B).
  • Observe the status of the bulb at the OUTPUT(Y).
  • Simultaneously, observe the internal structure and current flow in the NAND gate on the right side.
  • Also, Observe and verify the Truth table with respect to the states of the INPUT and OUTPUT switches.
  • Toggle Input switches and try different input combinations.
  • Repeat the experiment for n number of times for your clear understanding.
