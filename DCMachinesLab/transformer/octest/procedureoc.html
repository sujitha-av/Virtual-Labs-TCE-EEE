Open Circuit Test on Single Phase Transformer



Aim Apparatus Used Theory Procedure Formulae Simulator Quiz

Note!: Do not refresh the simulator until the experiment will being completed. Otherwise, you will lost your workdone!

Precautions:

  1. Variac on primary side should be in minimum position.
  2. Secondary winding of transformer should kept open.

Procedure:

  1. Understand the circuit connections given. Make sure all the precautions for the experiment were done.
  2. Select the transformer rating and suitable HV, LV configuration that you want to connect and fix it.
  3. Turn on the power supply.
  4. Adjust the variac gradually until the rated voltage.
  5. Note down the primary voltmeter, ammeter, wattmeter readings (by just adding it to the table).
  6. After taking the readings, decrease the variac to zero.
  7. Turn off the supply.
